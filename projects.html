2D Image Printer Robot

A robotic “dot-matrix style” drawing system that converts a digital image into a physical drawing on a cylindrical surface. The pipeline uses MATLAB for image processing (binary/edge-based dot representation) and Arduino-based motor control (stepper + servos) to coordinate cylinder rotation, pen translation, and pen contact.
